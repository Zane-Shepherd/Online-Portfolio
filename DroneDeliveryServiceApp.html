JAMZ Drone Delivery Service App

This design project was created in my first year of university. Our client was a startup company based out of the University of Ottawa named JAMZ. This company was attempting to start a drone-based food delivery business, and we were tasked with programming the front-end of their mobile app.

To create the application, our team researched visual design, and implemented these theories into the layout, colour palette, and overall appearance of the application. We then coded front-end of the application using JavaScript and CSS.
